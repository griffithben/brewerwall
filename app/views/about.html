What is Brewerwall?

Brewerwall is a hub in which data is combined into a single location and accessible by all. Either through RESTful services or through this site, anyone can take a look at elements used in brewing beer. Also provided are utilities that can be used in a variety of programming languages. Sourcecode is available on Github and can be easily installed through package managers like npm, composer and gem.
